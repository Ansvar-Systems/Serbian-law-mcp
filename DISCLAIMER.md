## Data Authority and Limitations

### Source Authority

This Tool aggregates data from official Serbian legal databases. However:

| Source Type | Authority Level | Use Case |
|------------|----------------|----------|
| **propisi.net** | Authoritative (consolidated) | Statute text, consolidated legislation (primary reference) |
| **Службени гласник РС (Official Gazette)** | Authoritative | Original official publication |
| **Community/open-data sources** | Supplementary | Cross-references, metadata (verify independently) |

**CRITICAL**: Community-maintained resources are **NOT** official government publications. While generally reliable, they may contain transcription errors, outdated information, or incomplete coverage.

### Commercial Legal Databases

For professional legal work, **always cross-check with commercial legal databases** such as:

- Paragraf Lex (paragraf.rs), Propisi Online

These services provide editorial oversight, comprehensive annotations (komentari), and professional-grade currency guarantees that this Tool **cannot** provide.

## Currency and Staleness

### No Real-Time Updates

- Database updates are **manual** and may lag official publications by **weeks or months**
- Serbian law changes continuously through new statutes (zakoni), amendments (izmene i dopune), and court decisions (sudske odluke)
- **Last-updated timestamps** in tool responses indicate data age, but should be treated as unreliable

### Staleness Warnings

The Tool includes staleness warnings when data is >30 days old. **These warnings are not exhaustive**:

- Even "fresh" data may be outdated if recent amendments were published in the Службени гласник Републике Србије
- Staleness warnings do not guarantee completeness or accuracy
- Always check official sources for the current legal position (važeće pravo)

### Amendments and Repeals

- A provision marked "in force" may have been:
  - Amended (izmenjen i dopunjen) after the last database update
  - Repealed (brisan / stavljen van snage) but not yet removed from the database
  - Subject to transitional rules (prelazne odredbe) affecting its applicability
- The Tool does **NOT** automatically track amendments in real-time

## Coverage Gaps

### What's Missing

This Tool does **NOT** include:

1. **Complete case law** -- Coverage of the Supreme Court of Cassation (Vrhovni kasacioni sud), Constitutional Court (Ustavni sud), and lower court decisions is limited
2. **Historical statute versions** -- Limited availability of historical provision wording (raniji tekst propisa)
3. **Legal commentary** -- No editorial annotations, academic commentary (pravna doktrina), or practice guides
4. **Unpublished decisions** -- Administrative rulings largely absent
5. **Full legislative history** -- Limited coverage of parliamentary materials (obrazloženja predloga zakona, zapisnici skupštinskih odbora)

### Impact on Professional Use

These gaps mean:

- **Incomplete Legal Research**: Tool results are inherently incomplete and may miss critical authorities
- **Context Missing**: Without commentary and legislative materials, interpretation may be incorrect
- **EU Accession Law**: Cannot fully assess the alignment of Serbian law with EU acquis communautaire during the accession process
